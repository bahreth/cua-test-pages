EP 147: Building Responsible AI Systems

December 12, 2024 - 54 min - Guest: Dr. Sarah Chen, NovaTech AI Research

In this episode, we explore the challenges and opportunities in building AI systems that are both powerful and responsible. Dr. Sarah Chen shares insights from her work leading NovaTech's AI Safety team, discussing practical approaches to alignment, robustness testing, and the future of human-AI collaboration.
