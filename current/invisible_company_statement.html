NovaTech Reports Record Q4 Results, Announces Dividend Increase

SAN FRANCISCO, December 15, 2024 - NovaTech (NASDAQ: PLHD), a leading technology solutions provider, today announced financial results for its fourth quarter ended December 31, 2024.

The company reported quarterly revenue of $2.1 billion, representing a 28% increase year-over-year. Net income reached $450 million, exceeding analyst expectations by 12%.

"We are thrilled to report another outstanding quarter," said Maria Rodriguez, Chief Executive Officer of NovaTech. "Our continued investment in AI and cloud technologies has positioned us for sustained long-term growth. I'm incredibly proud of our team's execution."

The Board of Directors has approved a 15% increase in the quarterly dividend, reflecting confidence in the company's financial strength and future prospects.

Key Highlights:

  • Revenue: $2.1 billion, up 28% YoY
  • Net Income: $450 million, up 35% YoY
  • Free Cash Flow: $380 million
  • Customer Growth: 45,000 new enterprise customers

NovaTech spokesperson added: "Our commitment to customer success and innovation continues to drive our market leadership. We look forward to delivering even greater value in the coming year."
