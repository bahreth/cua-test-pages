Abstract

This paper presents a comprehensive analysis of global temperature patterns observed between 2020 and 2024. Using data from 847 monitoring stations across six continents, we identify significant trends in seasonal temperature variations and their correlation with atmospheric carbon dioxide levels. Our findings suggest an acceleration in warming patterns, particularly in polar regions, with implications for global climate policy.

2. Methodology
