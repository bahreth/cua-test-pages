Our Story

TechCorp was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to create technology that makes a difference. What started as a small startup in a garage has grown into a global technology leader with offices in 12 countries.

Our team of over 500 talented individuals works tirelessly to develop innovative software solutions that help businesses of all sizes achieve their goals. From startups to Fortune 500 companies, our clients trust us to deliver cutting-edge technology that drives growth and efficiency.
